# Zalary Overview

Zalary is a confidential payroll dApp powered by Zama FHEVM. It demonstrates how employers can prepare payroll flows and how employees can claim payroll while preserving the idea of private salary allocations on public blockchain infrastructure.

## Why Confidential Payroll Matters

Payroll data is highly sensitive. Salary allocations, claim amounts, and payout timing can reveal compensation structures, employee relationships, and business operations. Public blockchains offer transparent settlement, but normal public values are not a good fit for private payroll data.

Zalary explores a payroll experience where the workflow remains auditable and wallet-native while sensitive values are represented through confidential FHEVM-oriented flows.

## What The Frontend Demonstrates

- Employer onboarding and dashboard entry.
- Payroll template creation.
- Employee list and salary allocation entry.
- Payroll run creation, funding, activation, and status review.
- Employee claim dashboard.
- Claim request, claim finalization, and withdrawal flow.
- Sepolia contract configuration for the deployed Zalary contracts.
